Technology you can trust

We are proud of our track record

With 55 patents pending and 9 already approved, we've built our indoor drone show system from the ground up with safety and reliability in mind. Our drones have traveled the world and have flown in more than 20 countries. Having toured with Metallica and Drake, and flown above audiences at corporate events, our technology has proven itself on the toughest testing grounds. A testament to the safety of our system, we've also flown in venues with no-drone policies. Our system is a complete all-round package you can rely on to deliver magic to your show.
